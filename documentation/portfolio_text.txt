Problem
The client operates a nationwide production network with continuous manufacturing operations, resulting in:
high electricity consumption costs
inconsistent power quality across facilities
aging electrical infrastructure in selected branches
electrical safety compliance risks
absence of structured benchmarking across sites
need to meet mandatory EPRA regulatory audit compliance requirements
The organization required a coordinated national programme to evaluate both energy performance and electrical system condition, while supporting submission readiness for EPRA compliance obligations.

Kinawa-supported engineering team implemented a structured multi-site Energy and Electrical Audit Programme covering 44 facilities, aligned with EPRA regulatory requirements and industry best practice methodologies.

Included:
facility-wide consumption analysis

generator performance review
tariff optimization assessment

branch-level benchmarking
site energy baseline development
